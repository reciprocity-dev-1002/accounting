Reciprocity Accounting · Identity Guide · V2 · February 2026
01
Brand Foundation
Purpose
To make timely financial insight usable for owners.
Financial information is only valuable if it is timely.
Core Belief Shift
Current Belief
"Accounting is compliance and my CPA handles it."

Review financials quarterly.
React to cash flow.
Rely on year-end tax prep.
Desired Belief
"Accounting is a monthly operating system."

Review dashboard monthly.
Adjust operations proactively.
Use numbers to guide decisions.
Value Pillars
01 — Timely Financials
Closed and delivered by the 10th of every month. Guaranteed.
02 — Connected Systems
Cloud-based and fully integrated. No spreadsheets, no disconnected tools, no PDF archaeology.
03 — Clear Performance Insights
So you run the business as confidently as you practice dentistry.
The Guarantee
Books closed by the 10th. Every month. Guaranteed.

Mark Logic
  • Two interlocking L-shapes that together complete a square. Neither is whole alone.
  • Bottom-left L = foundation (firm process). Top-right L = outcome (owner growth).
  • Mark reads bottom-left to top-right — numbers going up.
  • Gold square at center = the exchange point. Where reciprocity happens.
Clear Space
Minimum clear space = the width of the gold exchange square on all four sides of the mark.
