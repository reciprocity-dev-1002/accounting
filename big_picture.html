The Reciprocity Philosophy
We named the firm Reciprocity because the model only works when both sides contribute. Practice owners provide timely access, clear communication, and honest answers. We provide closed financials, clear reporting, and a monthly rhythm you can count on. That exchange is the foundation of everything we do.
Core Values
Honor the Exchange
We hold up our end and expect the same from our clients. When both sides follow through, the work stays on schedule and the numbers mean something.
Be Consistently Reliable
Our clients plan around our deliverables. We treat every deadline like it matters — because for a practice owner making real decisions, it does.
Choose Clarity Over Complexity
We don't send reports that require an accounting degree to read. Every deliverable is built to help an owner understand what happened and decide what to do next.
Practice Kindness Through Candor
If something looks off in the numbers, we say so early and directly. We'd rather have an honest conversation now than a difficult one later.
Three Uniques
01 — Closed by the 10th. Guaranteed.
Your financials are closed and delivered by the 10th of every month. If we miss it, your monthly fee is waived. It's in the engagement agreement, not just the marketing.
02 — A 5-Minute Monthly Review Video
Every month you get a short video walking through what changed, what's trending, and what to pay attention to. No jargon, no 40-page report — just the information you need to make decisions.
03 — Built Exclusively for Dental Practices
We don't serve restaurants, law firms, or general small businesses. Every workflow, benchmark, and report is designed around how dental practices actually operate.
Proven Process
Step 1 — Get Set Up
We collect everything we need to start: access to your accounting software, bank and credit card connections, payroll reports, and a production export from your practice management system. By the end of onboarding, we have a complete picture of your practice's finances and a clear schedule for what happens every month going forward.
Step 2 — Close and Deliver
Each month, our team closes your accounting by the 10th. You receive clean financials and a short review video that explains what changed, what's on track, and what needs your attention.
Step 3 — Review, Decide, Repeat
You use the reporting to make informed decisions about your practice — staffing, spending, growth, compensation. We answer questions as they come up. The cycle repeats every month with the same structure and the same standard.
